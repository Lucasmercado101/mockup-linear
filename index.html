    Main Features

    Issue tracking optimized for speed

    With Linear, we are creating an issue tracker with an unrivalled user experience. Linear is optimized for speed,
    efficiency and performance. Its realtime sync architecture makes it blazingly fast. Its minimalistic, purposeful
    design helps your team achieve more.

    Meet your command line

    Hit ⌘K to bring up the context-sensitive menu. Edit an issue's status, change assignees, modify
    subscriptions and a lot more in mere seconds

    Integrations

    Built for your workflow

    To help improve your personal and your teams' productivity. Linear ties into your existing tools, services and
    workflow

    • GitHub

      Link PRs and issues, automatically
      close issues on merge.

    • Slack

      Create issues from slack and get
      notifications on updates.

    • CLI

      Look up existing issues or create
      new ones right from your terminal.
